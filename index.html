About JIVA

Jiva EMR is India’s first intelligent Online AI based Healthcare platform which enables quick access to patient records for more coordinated, efficient care. Securely sharing electronic information with patients and other clinicians.

Helping providers (Doctors/clinics/hospitals) more effectively diagnose patients, reduce medical errors, and provide safer care and also enabling to select preferred pharmacies and diagnosis center for faster insurance reimbursement.

Integrated with Deep Data Analytics and leveraging the early diagnosis or prevention by detecting the below:

BMI Calculator

The body mass index (BMI) provides essential medical information related to body weight for the treatment and prognosis prediction of diseases such as cardiovascular disease, diabetes, and stroke. We propose a method for the prediction of normal, overweight, and obese classes based only on the combination of voice features that are associated with BMI status, independently of weight and height measurements.

Breast Cancer Detection

A deep learning tool that can spot metastatic (advanced) breast cancer with a greater accuracy than pathologists when looking at slides. We have two different type of system with greater accuracy.

Diabetes Detection

This leverages AI, advanced analytics, and diabetes technology to continually evaluate and update individuals about their glucose levels so they can make adjustments to their food intake, insulin dosages, daily routines, and other factors. It also classifies the patient.

Heart Disease Detection

Predicting heart diseases using data mining techniques, analyze the various combinations of mining algorithms used and conclude which techniques are effective and efficient.

Disease Detection using Symptoms

This predicts the disease according to the symptoms given by the user. This AI Based model is very crucial for early awareness of any disease.

Diabetic Retinopathy

This assess the role of artificial intelligence (AI)-based automated model for detection of diabetic retinopathy (DR) and sight-threatening DR (STDR) by fundus photography and validate it against ophthalmologist’s grading.
